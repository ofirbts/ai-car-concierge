# Shipping and Delivery

## Delivery Zones

- **Local (within 50 mi):** complimentary delivery within 5 business days.
- **Regional (51–500 mi):** flat rate **$499**, 7–10 business days.
- **National (501+ mi):** quote based on distance; typical **$899–$1,499**, 10–14 business days.

## Process

1. Vehicle is detailed and inspected before departure.
2. Enclosed transport for vehicles over **$75,000** list price.
3. Customer signs delivery acceptance at handoff; odometer and condition are documented.

## Tracking

You receive carrier contact and estimated window **24 hours** before arrival.
