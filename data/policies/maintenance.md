# Maintenance — Service Schedules

## Gasoline and Hybrid

- First service: **5,000 miles** or **6 months** (whichever comes first).
- Routine service: every **7,500 miles** or **12 months**.
- Major service (fluids, filters, inspection): every **30,000 miles**.

## Electric (EV) and Plug-in Hybrid

- EV annual check: **12 months** (battery health, software, brakes).
- Cabin filter: every **15,000 miles** or **12 months**.
- Brake fluid: every **24 months** (regenerative braking reduces wear but fluid still ages).

## Warranty

New vehicles include a **3-year / 36,000-mile** bumper-to-bumper warranty unless noted on the listing.
