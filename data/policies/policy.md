# Sales Policy — Model Year Eligibility

## 2022+ Sales Policy

Premium Auto Gallery sells **only** vehicles from model year **2022 or newer**.

Vehicles in our inventory with a model year **before 2022** are classified as **Pending De-listing**. They may appear in search results for transparency, but they **cannot** be sold, reserved, or purchased through this dealership.

If a customer asks about a 2020 or 2021 vehicle:

- Acknowledge that the vehicle may still appear in stock records.
- Explain clearly that it **cannot be sold** under the 2022+ Sales Policy.
- Offer to help find a comparable 2022+ alternative.

## Pending De-listing

Any inventory record with `year < 2022` must be treated as **Pending De-listing** regardless of `stock_count`.
