# Support — Refunds and Contact

## Refunds

- Full refund within **7 calendar days** of delivery if the vehicle has fewer than **500 miles** and passes return inspection.
- Deposits on reservations are refundable within **48 hours** if the reservation is cancelled before contract signing.
- Custom-ordered vehicles are non-refundable after factory order confirmation.
