# FAQs — Test Drives and General

## Scheduling a Test Drive

1. Choose a vehicle from inventory (2022+ eligible only).
2. Call (555) 100-2000 or use chat to request a slot.
3. Bring a valid driver license and proof of insurance.
4. Test drives are **30 minutes**, by appointment, Mon–Sat 10:00–17:00.

Same-day test drives may be available if the vehicle is on the lot and not reserved.

## Financing

We partner with select lenders; pre-qualification does not affect credit score.
